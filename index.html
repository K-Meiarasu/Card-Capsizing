MY FAVORITE ANIME CHARACTER

ZENITSU AGASTUMA

Zenitsu is a powerful member of the Demon Slayer Corps. He holds great respect and admiration towards his comrades and his deceased teacher, Jigoro Kuwajima, and his inner focus and drive in avenging him always help to wash away his cowardice and second thoughts when he's in a pinch.

  • Lightning-based Swordsmanship
  • Total Concentration Constant
  • Superhuman speed and sense of hearing
  • Berserker power upon sleepwalking
